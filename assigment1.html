My Favourite Book : "The Great Gatsby"

"The Great Gatsby" is a novel written by F.Scott Fitzgeraldand was first published in 1925.It is


widely considered one of the greatest American novels.The story is set during the Roaring Twentiesand

explores themes of love, wealth, and the American Dream.


The novel's main character, Jay Gatsby, is famous for his extravagant parties and unrequired love for

Daisy Buchanan. His story ultimately leads to tragic consequences. The book is known for its

symbolism and its critical view of the American Dream.
